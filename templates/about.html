What is an ATS?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by companies to manage the recruitment process. Over 90% of Fortune 500 companies use ATS to filter and rank resumes before they ever reach human recruiters.

These systems scan resumes for specific keywords, formatting, and structure. If your resume isn't optimized for ATS, it might be rejected automaticallyâ€”even if you're the perfect candidate.
